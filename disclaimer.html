Who I Am

Hi, I’m Naijian Lin — a certified graduate of the Institute for Integrative Nutrition (IIN) and a holistic health coach based in California. My background began in the corporate world, where I spent years working long hours with little balance. Over time, I realized I wanted to help others improve their energy, relationships, and overall well-being through practical, compassionate coaching.

This website is my personal blog — a space where I share my honest reflections on health coaching, learning experiences, and the lessons I gained during my studies at IIN. Everything here is written from my own perspective as a former student and practicing health coach.

My Mission

My goal is simple: to share real experiences, helpful resources, and inspiration for people who are exploring health coaching or looking to make positive lifestyle changes. I believe wellness is not about perfection — it’s about progress, awareness, and kindness to ourselves and others.
